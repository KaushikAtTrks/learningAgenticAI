Title: Applications of Agentic AI in Real-World Systems

Abstract:
This paper reviews practical applications of Agentic AI, including robotics, virtual assistants, and autonomous vehicles. We analyze how agentic principles enhance system performance and user experience.

Keywords: Agentic AI, robotics, virtual assistants, autonomous vehicles

Introduction:
Agentic AI systems are increasingly deployed in real-world scenarios, demonstrating improved autonomy and adaptability. This paper surveys key applications and their impact on technology and society.

Conclusion:
Agentic AI is transforming industries by enabling intelligent, self-directed systems that interact seamlessly with humans and environments.
