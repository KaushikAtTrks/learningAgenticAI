Title: Future Trends in Agentic AI Research

Abstract:
This paper explores emerging trends in Agentic AI, such as explainable agents, human-agent collaboration, and scalable agentic systems. We discuss open challenges and opportunities for future research.

Keywords: Agentic AI, future trends, explainable AI, human-agent collaboration, scalability

Introduction:
Agentic AI is rapidly evolving, with new research directions focusing on transparency, collaboration, and scalability. This paper identifies promising areas for innovation and growth in agentic technologies.

Conclusion:
Future research in Agentic AI will shape the next generation of intelligent systems, emphasizing explainability, collaboration, and large-scale deployment.
