Title: Ethical Considerations in Agentic AI

Abstract:
This paper examines the ethical challenges posed by Agentic AI, including accountability, transparency, and bias. We propose frameworks for responsible development and deployment of agentic systems.

Keywords: Agentic AI, ethics, accountability, transparency, bias

Introduction:
As Agentic AI systems gain autonomy, ethical concerns become paramount. This paper discusses the risks and responsibilities associated with agentic decision-making and offers guidelines for ethical AI design.

Conclusion:
Addressing ethical issues in Agentic AI is essential for fostering trust and ensuring positive societal impact.
