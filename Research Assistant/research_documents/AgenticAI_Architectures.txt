Title: Agentic AI Architectures and Algorithms

Abstract:
This paper provides an overview of architectures and algorithms used in Agentic AI, including reinforcement learning, multi-agent systems, and hierarchical planning. We highlight recent advances and future research directions.

Keywords: Agentic AI, architectures, algorithms, reinforcement learning, multi-agent systems

Introduction:
Agentic AI relies on sophisticated architectures and algorithms to achieve autonomy and adaptability. This paper reviews key technical approaches and their applications in agentic systems.

Conclusion:
Innovative architectures and algorithms are driving the evolution of Agentic AI, enabling more capable and intelligent agents.
